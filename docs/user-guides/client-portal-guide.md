# Client Portal User Guide

## Overview

The Immigration AI Client Portal is a secure, user-friendly platform that allows clients to manage their immigration information, upload documents, and track their case progress. This guide provides step-by-step instructions for using all features of the client portal.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Navigate to the Client Portal**
   - Visit the Immigration AI website
   - Click on "Client Sign Up" tab

2. **Fill in Your Information**
   - **Personal Details**: Enter your first and last name
   - **Email Address**: Use a valid email address (this will be your login username)
   - **Phone Number**: Optional but recommended for contact purposes
   - **Password**: Create a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to confirm

3. **Create Account**
   - Click "Create Account" button
   - You will be automatically logged in upon successful registration

### Logging In

1. **Access the Login Page**
   - Visit the Immigration AI website
   - Click on "Sign In" tab

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - Email address (used during registration)
   - Password

3. **Access Your Portal**
   - Click "Sign In" to access your client portal

## Portal Features

### My Profile

The profile section allows you to manage all your personal and immigration-related information.

#### Personal Information
- **Name**: First and last name
- **Email**: Your registered email address (cannot be changed)
- **Phone**: Contact phone number
- **Address**: Complete current address information

#### Immigration Details
- **Date of Birth**: Your birth date
- **Nationality**: Your current nationality
- **Country of Birth**: Where you were born
- **Passport Number**: Current passport details
- **Immigration Status**: Your current status in the country

#### Emergency Contact
- **Contact Name**: Name of emergency contact person
- **Contact Phone**: Emergency contact phone number
- **Relationship**: Relationship to you (spouse, parent, etc.)

#### Editing Your Profile
1. Navigate to "My Profile" tab
2. Click "Edit Profile" button
3. Update any information as needed
4. Click "Save Changes" to save your updates
5. Click "Cancel" to discard changes

### Immigration Information

This section allows you to provide detailed information about your background for your immigration case.

#### Education History
Add your educational qualifications and background:

1. **Click "Add Education Entry"**
2. **Fill in Details**:
   - Institution name
   - Degree/qualification obtained
   - Field of study
   - Start and end dates
   - Country where you studied

3. **Save Entry**: Information is saved when you click "Save All Information"
4. **Remove Entry**: Click the trash icon to remove an entry

#### Work History
Document your employment background:

1. **Click "Add Work Entry"**
2. **Fill in Details**:
   - Company name
   - Position/job title
   - Start and end dates
   - Country where you worked
   - Job description and responsibilities

3. **Save Entry**: Information is saved when you click "Save All Information"
4. **Remove Entry**: Click the trash icon to remove an entry

#### Travel History
Record your international travel:

1. **Click "Add Travel Entry"**
2. **Fill in Details**:
   - Country visited
   - Purpose of visit (tourism, business, education, etc.)
   - Entry and exit dates
   - Additional notes about the trip

3. **Save Entry**: Information is saved when you click "Save All Information"
4. **Remove Entry**: Click the trash icon to remove an entry

#### Additional Information
- **Languages**: List languages you speak (comma-separated)
- **Additional Notes**: Any other relevant information for your case

#### Saving Your Information
- Click "Save All Information" button at the top of the page
- All sections (education, work, travel, additional info) are saved together
- You'll receive a confirmation message when information is saved successfully

### My Documents

The documents section allows you to upload, manage, and download your immigration documents.

#### Uploading Documents

1. **Click "Upload Document"**
2. **Select File**:
   - Choose a file from your computer
   - Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG
   - Maximum file size: 10MB

3. **Choose Document Type**:
   - Passport
   - Birth Certificate
   - Marriage Certificate
   - Diploma/Degree
   - Employment Letter
   - Financial Statement
   - Other

4. **Add Notes** (Optional):
   - Add any relevant notes about the document

5. **Upload**:
   - Click "Upload" to save the document
   - You'll see a confirmation when upload is complete

#### Managing Documents

**Document Library**:
- View all your uploaded documents in a table format
- See document name, type, size, verification status, and upload date

**Document Status**:
- **Pending Review**: Document has been uploaded but not yet verified
- **Verified**: Document has been reviewed and approved by your immigration agency

**Downloading Documents**:
- Click the download button (arrow icon) next to any document
- The file will be downloaded to your computer

#### Document Types and Requirements

**Passport**:
- Clear, high-quality scan of your passport photo page
- Ensure all text is readable

**Birth Certificate**:
- Official birth certificate from issuing authority
- If not in English, include certified translation

**Marriage Certificate**:
- Official marriage certificate
- Include certified translation if not in English

**Educational Documents**:
- Diplomas, degrees, transcripts
- Include credential evaluation if required

**Employment Documents**:
- Employment letters, contracts, pay stubs
- Should include job title, duties, and employment dates

**Financial Documents**:
- Bank statements, tax returns, proof of funds
- Ensure personal information is visible but secure

## Security and Privacy

### Data Protection
- All your information is encrypted and securely stored
- Only you and your assigned immigration agency can access your data
- Your information is never shared with unauthorized parties

### Account Security
- Use a strong, unique password for your account
- Log out when finished, especially on shared computers
- Contact support immediately if you suspect unauthorized access

### Data Isolation
- You can only access your own information
- The system prevents access to other clients' data
- All data access is logged for security purposes

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Cannot Log In**:
- Verify your email address and password are correct
- Check if Caps Lock is on
- Try resetting your password if needed

**Upload Fails**:
- Check file size (must be under 10MB)
- Verify file format is supported
- Ensure stable internet connection

**Information Not Saving**:
- Check all required fields are filled
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Try refreshing the page and attempting again

**Cannot Access Certain Features**:
- Verify you're logged in with the correct account
- Some features may require additional setup by your agency

### Getting Help

1. **Check This Guide**: Review relevant sections for step-by-step instructions
2. **Contact Your Agency**: Reach out to your immigration agency for case-specific questions
3. **Technical Support**: Contact technical support for system-related issues

### Error Messages

**"Access Denied"**: You don't have permission to access this feature
**"File Too Large"**: Your file exceeds the 10MB limit
**"Invalid File Type"**: File format is not supported
**"Network Error"**: Check your internet connection and try again
